About Me

Who am I?

My name is Manolo Martin and I am a junior web developer based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I currently am studying computer science at George Mason University in Fairfax, Virginia, and plan to pursue a career in software engineering. I study and take on projects every day in my pursuit of this goal.

What can I do?

Even though I am a junior web developer, I have experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java! You can check out some of my projects on my Projects page or check out everything I've worked on at Github!

What motivates me?

I always had an interest in all things technology, which is why I enjoy programming and development. Watching all of the building blocks come together to is something satisfying no matter how much I struggled to build it, and the more I build the better I'll get!
